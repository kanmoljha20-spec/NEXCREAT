Eligibility Criteria

  • Pass a specific subscriber milestone:
    • Silver: 100,000 subscribers
    • Gold: 1,000,000 subscribers
    • Diamond: 10,000,000 subscribers
    • Red Diamond: 100,000,000 subscribers
  • Channel active within last 6 months
  • No active Community Guidelines violation in last 365 days
  • Compliance with YouTube Terms of Service
  • No rejection/suspension from YouTube Partner Program
  • Channel not terminated or linked to terminated account
  • No deceptive, spammy, or scam content
  • Main content must be original
  • Awards are personal use only; violation may lead to disciplinary action
